## Maple Court World Plan

Canvas: 8192×8192 px, 32px tiles (256×256). Bounds below are targets — snap to the tile grid and adjust ±2 tiles for clean roads. Region borders are always physical (tree lines, water, terrain), never invisible walls. Every region gets a name banner on entry and a minimap block. Unbuilt regions exist as meadow + roads + a teaser sign.

Future systems: Swimming — player enters water at the lake, slow movement penalty (wade zone), Biscuit won't follow past the dock; tidal shimmer visual effect; reserved for a future session.

Connective tissue: creek flows from Woods south into the Lake; bike paths link Construction → Woods → Park → Lake; the main road net links Neighborhood ↔ School ↔ Shopping ↔ Lake boardwalk. Storm drain entrances: grates near Park, Neighborhood center, and School road.

Modes and where they live: Dog Days Dash (whole map, home → playground); E-Bike Grand Prix (starts Shopping center; separate pseudo-3D race view); Woods Obstacle Course (creek crossings, future); Meetup hub (Treehouse village, future).
